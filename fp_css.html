Color Wheel

The circles closest to the user are the primary colors: red, green, and blue. These circles have images that relate to their respective potion of the color wheel: red—berries, green—space, and blue—water. Each circle is labeled with its text in its complements color.

The circles just beneath the primary colors are the secondary colors: yellow, cyan, and magenta. These circles have images that relate to their respective potion of the color wheel: yellow—flowers, cyan— day sky, magenta—night sky. Each circle is labeled with its text in its complements color.

The lowest circle is the color wheel, which has every color within it.
